Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

MyMilesAI ("we", "our", "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy describes how we handle your personal information when you use our GPS mileage tracking application.

1. Information We Collect

Location Data: GPS coordinates are collected solely for mileage tracking purposes. This data is used to calculate trip distances, detect driving automatically, and generate mileage reports.

Trip Data: Start/end locations, distances, timestamps, category classifications, and trip notes.

Profile Data: Your name (optional), locale preference, and app settings.

2. How We Store Your Data

All trip data is stored locally on your device using encrypted browser localStorage. Your data stays on your device unless you explicitly:

  • Enable cloud sync (via JSONBin.io — your data is stored in a private, encrypted bin)
  • Export data as CSV, PDF, or JSON backup
  • Use the AI assistant (your query and trip context are sent to Anthropic's API)

3. Third-Party Services

  • Google Maps Platform: Used for address autocomplete, distance calculation, and map display. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Anthropic (Claude AI): When you use the AI assistant, your query and anonymized trip data are sent to Anthropic's API. Requires your own API key. Subject to Anthropic's Privacy Policy.
  • JSONBin.io: Optional cloud sync service. Trip data is stored in private bins. Subject to JSONBin's privacy policy.

4. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal contacts or address books
  • Browsing history
  • Photos, files, or media
  • Financial or payment information
  • We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties for advertising

5. Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • Export: Download all your data as CSV, PDF, or JSON at any time from Settings
  • Delete: Clear all data using the "Sign Out" option in Settings
  • Portability: JSON backup includes all trips, settings, and preferences for device migration

6. GDPR & CCPA Compliance

For users in the EU (GDPR) and California (CCPA):

  • You have the right to access, correct, and delete your personal data
  • You can request a copy of all data we hold about you
  • Data processing is based on your explicit consent (granting location permission)
  • You can withdraw consent at any time by revoking location permissions

7. Data Security

Trip data stored on your device is encrypted using AES encryption. Cloud-synced data is transmitted over HTTPS. API keys are stored locally and never transmitted to our servers.

8. Children's Privacy

MyMilesAI is not intended for use by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or your data, contact us at: privacy@mymilesai.com
